Rate interpretation guide
≥ 55% — Strong. Payer paying well; low patient balance leakage.
40–54% — Acceptable. Verify patient balance follow-up is complete.
25–39% — Below average. Possible denial pattern or high write-off rate.
Below 25% — Critical. Likely systematic denial or contract issue.
