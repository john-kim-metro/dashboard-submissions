New provider slots are not fully booked from day one — the practice intentionally ramps scheduling capacity alongside panel growth. However, some providers also self-limit patient volume during early months independent of scheduling constraints. The ramp standard distinguishes these cases: a provider with 140+ scheduled hours but low VPH is not capacity-limited by design — they are underperforming their available schedule.
