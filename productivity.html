3 — Rising provider VPH velocity (quarterly, Q2 2024 – Q1 2026)
Quarterly VPH trajectory for newer providers · click a label to isolate
System average shown as dashed red reference line. Q2 2024 = May–Jun only (Apr excluded).
Fastest ramp
FO (Ortiz) — system avg in 1 quarter
Joined Q2 '25 at 3.53 VPH. Crossed system average by Q3 '25 (5.91) and has held 6.0+ for three consecutive quarters — fastest and most sustained ramp in the cohort.
Steady growth
SY, CP — consistent climb, not peaked
Yu (SY) grew from 2.68 to 4.49 VPH over 8 quarters with no down quarter since Q4 '24. Park Chohee (CP) broke above 4.5 in Q4 '25 and is holding. Neither has plateaued.
Step-change
RH (Huh) — slow start, sharp jump
Spent Q2–Q4 '24 below 3.5 VPH (minimal early hours). Jumped to 5.1 in Q3 '25 and held near 5.0 since. Quarterly data confirms this was structural, not a one-month spike.
On track
CH (Huang) — linear slope since Q1 '25
Four consecutive quarters of improvement: 1.1 → 2.1 → 2.8 → 3.3 → 4.1 VPH. Consistent ~0.8 VPH/quarter slope. On pace to reach system average by Q3 '26.
Stalled
GG & PS — flat for 3+ quarters
Garcia (GG) averaged 2.39 VPH across Q3 '25, Q4 '25, Q1 '26 with no upward trend. Sit (PS) similar at 2.8–3.2. Neither fits a normal ramp curve after 9+ months.
Benchmark
System avg ~5.0–5.4 VPH
FO and RH are at or above. SY and CP approaching. CH on track. GG and PS are 2.5–2.7 points below with no convergence trajectory visible.
